Section 02 · The Path

Three sessions. One clear journey.

Step 01

Build the Foundation

Demystify LLMs. Understand how they think, what they know, and why they sometimes confidently get things wrong.

Step 02

Get Hands On

Open the tools. Try real prompts on real tasks. See the difference between a weak prompt and one that works every time.

Step 03

Find Your Fit

Map AI into your actual work. Identify which tasks are worth automating first and how to start without breaking anything.

Step 04

Leave With a Plan

Walk out with a specific, realistic AI action plan for your role — not a list of apps to download but a sequence of moves to make.
