Section 01 · What to Expect

Walk in curious.
Leave capable.

This workshop is built for smart people who haven't had time to figure out AI yet. We go slow enough to actually understand things, and fast enough that you leave with real skills.

01

How AI actually works

No mystery, no magic. You'll understand what an LLM is, why it behaves the way it does, and what it can and can't do.

02

Prompts that get results

Most people get mediocre output because they ask mediocre questions. You'll learn how to communicate with AI so it does useful work.

03

Your first real automation

Connect tools together, eliminate a repetitive task, and see what it feels like when AI handles something you used to do manually.

04

A framework you'll keep using

You won't just learn tools. You'll leave with a way of thinking about AI and automation that works for anything that comes next.

05

Hands-on the whole time
